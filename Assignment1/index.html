GRAND FURNITURE

Grand Furniture began as a small, one-store operation in downtown Norfolk, in 1947. Founded by Rose Stein, and assisted by her two sons, Grand quickly gained the reputation as a store where customers could get both quality home furnishings and honest value. These factors have been the cornerstone upon which Grand Furniture has grown. Today, the 4th generation continues this tradition.
